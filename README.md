## 1. UTC (Cayman) Fund Performance Dashboard
**Tools:** Power BI, Power Query, Excel
* **Project Overview:** Reconstructed the **UTC (Cayman) SPC Ltd. 2024 Annual Report** into an interactive dashboard.
* **Financial Logic:** Tracked **$7.4M in Assets Under Management (AUM)** and analyzed **NAV recovery trends**.
* **Key Features:** Built automated data cleaning pipelines in **Power Query** to handle complex financial statement formatting.

## 2. HR Analytics & Workforce Dashboard
**Tools:** Power BI, Power Query, DAX
* **Objective:** Analyzed 161 employees to identify workforce trends, salary distributions, and leave balance risks.
* **Key Visuals:** Built a **Scatter Plot** for salary vs. qualification and a **Cumulative Headcount** line chart to show company growth.
* **Interactive Features:** Implemented **Small Multiples** and Tooltips to see "LBL over 20 days" per job title.
